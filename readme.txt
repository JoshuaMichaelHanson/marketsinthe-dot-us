Hello and thank you for choosing marketsinthe.us

1.  Unzip into your xampp/htdocs folder
2.  Navigate to finalProject_hansonJosh or use the website
		marketsinthe.us
3.	You are now on the index.html, this is the homepage
4.	On the menu Click Map
5.	If you device has geo-location, choose yes to share your location
6.	You now see the closest farmers markets in your area.
7.	Explore and when you find one you like add to your favorites
8.	Click on Favorite Markets on the menu
9.	Now you see all your favorites.  Click one and add a vendor.
10.	You can grab the markets and arrange them according to your preferences.

Thanks Again!!
